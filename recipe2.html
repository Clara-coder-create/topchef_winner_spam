Spam Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 can of spam
  • Soy sauce, sake, mirin, sugar
  • Two eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Rice, cooked
  • Green onion, chopped

Recipe

  1. Cut the spam into 1/2 inch cubes.
  2. Combine soy sauce, sake, mirin, and sugar in a mixing bowl
  3. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt in another mixing bowl.
  4. Heat a pan over high heat, add 1 tbsp oil and pour beaten eggs. Scramble the egg to cook evenly. When the eggâ€™s cooked 3/4 way, remove from the pan and set aside.
  5. Heat the pan over high heat then add 3 tbsp oil and spam. Cook the spam until all the edges are golden brown, about 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in chopped garlic, cook for 1 minute.
  7. Add the cooked rice and sauce mixture.
  8. Add the egg and break down into the rice.
  9. When the egg is mixed well, turn off the heat and stir in green onion.

Enjoy!
