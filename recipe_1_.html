Truly tasty spam

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp liquid smoke
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp water
  • Wrap the tofu in a dish towel and carefully squeze as much water out as you can without turning the tofu into mush. Cut the tofu into thin rectangular pieces.
  • In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, maple syrup, liquid smoke, mirin, ground ginger, garlic powder and water. Mix well.
  • Get a flat container, lay the tofu pieces flat and pour in the marinade. Each piece of tofu should be covered in marinade. Let it stand for 15 min, then turn the tofu pieces and let stand for another 15 min. If you're in a hurry one side only is okay too.
  • Put the tofu on a lined baking sheet and bake for 15 min at 375 F.
  • After 15 min, flip each piece of tofu and return the tray to the oven for another 15 min.
